    The Privacy Problem We're Solving

    People use dozens of apps daily without knowing how much data is collected, leaving them vulnerable to tracking and profiling.

    Complex Policies

    Privacy policies are long, unclear, and often ignored by users who just want to use the app.

    Limited Control

    Most users don't know how to opt out, limit tracking, or delete their accounts and data.

    Vulnerability

    This leads to loss of control over personal data and increased risk of leaks or misuse.

    Our Solution

    TrackFree.me is a free, privacy-first web tool that empowers users to understand their digital footprint and take control of it.

    App Search & Selection

    Users can quickly search for or select popular apps from a preloaded list to see how each one handles their data.

    Data Collection Overview

    Clearly displays what types of personal data the app collects — such as location, contacts, browsing history, or biometrics.

    Actionable Guidance

    Offers step-by-step instructions on how users can reduce tracking, adjust settings, or delete their account entirely.
